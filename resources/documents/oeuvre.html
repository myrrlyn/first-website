I write almost solely for or in the Elder Scrolls universe, specifically on the topics of the Orcs, background metaphysics of the Aurbis, and occasionally stories of Tamrielic life. I am also working on a construction of “Orsinium Novum” – my take on the Orcs’ new home in the Skyrim–Hammerfell border, as well as a general lore compendium about the Orsimer (a Pocket Guide to Orsinium, if you will), though neither of these projects are moving quickly.
